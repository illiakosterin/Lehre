Data Science with Python is a structured, end-to-end curriculum that takes students from the fundamentals of the Python language through to deploying machine learning models in production. Every concept is grounded in finance applications — datasets, case studies, and problems are drawn from real markets, giving students a direct line between what they learn and how it gets used.

The course is designed to build both depth and breadth. Early modules establish strong foundations in data manipulation and statistical thinking, while later modules push into deep learning, natural language processing, and model deployment with FastAPI and Streamlit. The progression is deliberate — each module builds on the last, so by the end students can take a raw dataset and turn it into a deployed, interactive application.

Learning is reinforced at every step through 18+ interactive Colab notebooks, 40+ practice problems per module, and quizzes after each lesson. Module handouts run 100+ pages and are supplemented by reading companions and in-class worksheets, making the material accessible both as a live course and as a standalone self-study resource.
